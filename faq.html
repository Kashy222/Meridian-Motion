FAQ

Frequently Ask Question

Do I need a doctor's referral for physiotherapy?

No, you do not need a doctor's referral to see a physiotherapist. However, some insurance providers may require one for coverage purposes.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your specific condition, severity, and how your body responds to treatment. We will provide a personalized plan after your initial assessment.

How long does it take to recover?

Recovery time varies greatly depending on the injury, your overall health, and adherence to prescribed exercises. We prioritize safe, effective recovery over quick fixes.

Can I get therapy at home?

Currently, we offer primarily in-clinic treatments at our Kalyan location to ensure you have access to our full range of specialized equipment. Please contact us for specific case discussions.

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of musculoskeletal issues, sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation needs, and postural or ergonomic problems.

What should I expect on my first visit?

Your first visit will include a comprehensive physical assessment, discussion of your medical history, and the development of a personalized treatment plan.

Is treatment painful or uncomfortable?

We are committed to providing the highest standard of care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our goal is to help you recover faster and stronger.
