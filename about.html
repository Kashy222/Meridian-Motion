About Us

Our Team

We’re Here to Support Your Recovery Every Step of the Way

Meridian Motion was born out of a shared passion to help people move better and live pain-free. As a husband-and-wife team, we combined our distinct specializations to offer an integrated approach to healing. Our clinic in Kalyan has grown into a trusted space for comprehensive musculoskeletal, sports, and manual therapy, all focused on patient recovery and well-being.

 Our Commitment

At Meridian Motion, we are dedicated to providing personalized care that goes beyond surface-level symptoms. We focus on identifying the root cause of your pain or dysfunction to deliver lasting results.

 Our Vision

To be the most trusted and effective physiotherapy and sports rehabilitation clinic in Kalyan, empowering our community to live active, pain-free lives.

 Our Mission

To provide evidence-based, compassionate care combining the latest techniques in musculoskeletal and sports physiotherapy with advanced therapies like dry needling and Kinesio taping.

What Makes Us Different

More than therapy - it's about trust, warmth, and real recovery.

We understand that every body is unique. That's why we take a tailored, holistic approach to healing, ensuring you receive the exact care you need.

Certified & Experienced Therapists

Our clinic is run by highly qualified specialists with extensive training in musculoskeletal rehabilitation, sports therapy, and advanced manual techniques.

 Personalized treatment

Every patient receives a fully customized treatment protocol tailored to their unique needs and lifestyle.

  • Individualized assessments
  • Evidence-based interventions
  • Progressive recovery plans

Home care services

 Long-term recovery
